Linear Equation


A linear equation is any equation that can be written in the from

ax + b = 0

where a and b are real number and x is a variable. This from sometimes called the standard from of a linear equation. Not that most linear equation will not start off this from. Also, the variable may or may not be an x s don't get too locked into always seeing an x there.

Intersection

The intersection of two sets A and B, denoted by A ⋂ B, is the set of all objects ther are members of bots the sets A and B. In symbols,

A ⋂ B = { x : x ∈ A and x ∈ B}
